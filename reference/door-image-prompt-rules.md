# Door Image Generation — Prompt Rules

Standard rules for generating accurate RSNE door images via Gemini Flash.

## Terminology
1. Door **plug** = the inner, functional part of the door (the panel that swings)
2. Door **frame** = the stationary part attached to the wall; hinges, closer arm, and strike mount to the frame
3. **Hinges** = components attached to the edge of the door that enable the swing
4. **Latches** = components that allow the door to remain closed/secure and the handle to open
5. **"Hinge Left"** = hinges on the left side. **"Hinge Right"** = hinges on the right side

## Standard Door Appearance
6. Standard door and frame should have a **white finish**
7. Frame should be **6" wide** and match door color
8. RSNE logo is always **centered at the top 1/3** of the door plug

## Door Dimensions (Standard 3' x 7')
9. Door plug is **36" wide x 84" tall**
10. All hardware must be proportioned correctly to these dimensions

## Hardware Specifications & Placement
11. **Dent D690 Hinge**: 10"L x 3.5"H — flat oval strap, brushed satin stainless, visible screw holes
12. **Dent D90 Latch/Handle**: 13"L — mounted on latch side at mid-height
13. **Dent 275 Closer**: 5"L — installed **vertically** at the **top corner on the same side as the latch**. On an 84" door, a 5" closer is very small. RSNE doors NEVER have a large horizontal hydraulic closer.

## Hinge Count & Position (3' x 7' doors)
14. **Exactly 2 hinges** — no middle hinge exists on a standard 3' x 7' cooler/freezer door
15. **Top hinge**: ~6" down from the top edge of the plug
16. **Bottom hinge**: ~6" up from the bottom edge of the plug (installed OVER the diamond plate kick-plate)
17. There is NO hinge in the middle of the door

## Diamond Plate
18. Diamond plate kick-plate is about **2' (24") tall** and the **full width of the plug**
19. Diamond plate is also installed on the **frame, on both sides adjacent to the plug's diamond plate**, at the same height

## Rendering Scope
20. All renderings should include the **plug, frame, and standard hardware ONLY**. Do NOT include switches, conduit, surrounding wall panels, extra framing, or any elements beyond the door assembly itself.

## Door Style
21. RSNE standard doors are NOT Bally-style (flush). The door plug sits **raised ~1.5" off the frame** due to magnetic gaskets around the perimeter.
22. At a 3/4 angle, the thickness/depth of the door panel protruding from the frame should be visible.
